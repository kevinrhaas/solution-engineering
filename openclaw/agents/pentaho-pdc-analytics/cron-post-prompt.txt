FORMATTING (critical — this is what separates posts people read from posts people skip):
- Use REAL NEWLINES in your content string. Write actual line breaks between paragraphs.
  Do NOT write the characters \n — just put real newlines in the string.
- Use 1-2 markdown headings (## or ###) to break the post into sections.
- Use **bold** sparingly for key phrases or the core insight.
- Use bullet points or numbered lists when comparing things or listing examples.
- Keep paragraphs to 3-4 sentences max. Short paragraphs are easier to read.
- Leave a blank line between every paragraph.
- DO NOT write one giant wall of text. Break it up.
